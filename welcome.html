Welcome to KMatch! 🎉

How to use:

  1. Visit LinkedIn Jobs in the Netherlands -> click https://www.linkedin.com/jobs/collections/
  2. Look for the KM badge on job listings - it means the job is from a recognized sponsor
  3. Click on the extension icon to see all jobs with their sponsor status

Features:

  • 🔍 Highlights jobs from recognized sponsors
  • 🔄 Maintains an up-to-date database
  • 🌐 Detects English language job title
